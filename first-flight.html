First Flight

September 4, 2025

Glider undergoing preflight checks

Moments before launch

Launch!

Flew an initial flight in the stratosphere. Unfortunately, a failure with the burn wire resulted in the vehicle not separating cleanly from the balloon.

Despite this, the vehicle operated nominally through ascent, remained stable through burst, and maintained communication until splashdown.

We subscribe to a "fail fast" philosophy — rapid iteration, aggressive testing, and real-world validation. This first flight provided critical data, and we are continuing to iterate quickly toward reliable autonomous recovery.
